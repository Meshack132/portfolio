About

Architectural Leadership

Boitumelo Mthimkhulu is a Senior Architectural Technologist with over 12 years of experience spanning commercial, retail, residential, and transport infrastructure projects across South Africa.

Currently serving as Programme Manager at PRASA CRES in the South Gauteng Region, Boitumelo has led the revival of 26 vandalized train stations and directed multiple refurbishment programmes, restoring commuter access.

With a Master of Architecture from WITS University and registration with SACAP, Boitumelo combines design excellence, technical rigor, and strategic programme management.
